Research Interests

Chen is interested in various weakly supervised learning problems in the context of computer vision, including self-supervised learning, semi-supervised learning and learning with noisy/coarse labels.

About

Chen is a Postdoctoral Research Associate at University College London, working with Prof. Miguel Rodrigues. He finished his PhD at Queen Mary University of London in UK, working with Prof. Ioannis Patras. He also visited University of Trento as an AI4Media Junior Exchange Fellow, working with Prof. Nicu Sebe. Before that, he received his bachelor's degree from Nankai University and master's degree from Tsinghua University.
