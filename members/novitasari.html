About

Maria is a Marie Skłodowska-Curie PhD fellow at UCL working on algorithms for multimodal data-based inference and prediction for climate science applications under the supervision of Professor Miguel Rodrigues. She obtained her master degree in computer science from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology where her research revolved around the combination of active learning and periodicity analysis.
